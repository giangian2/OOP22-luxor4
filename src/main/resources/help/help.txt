This is Luxor Game Guide!

You can choose on which level you want to start by clicking on the button "levels".
Then you can start the game.

The goal of the game is to eliminate all the balls before
they enter in the final hole. 
To eliminate them, there has to be 3 or more balls of the same color.

You will use a cannon to shot at them (BUTTON SPACE).
The cannon can move to the right (RIGHT ARROW) and to the left (LEFT ARROW).

If you want to stop the game, just click the "P" key.

Good luck!
